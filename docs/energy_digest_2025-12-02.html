Pramac EMS features special modes for C&I battery storage sites

Source: PV Magazine

Summary

Pramac's EMS (Energy Management System) is a smart solution for commercial and industrial battery storage sites, offering advanced modes such as "Smart Self-Consumption" and "External Control". These modes enable optimized charging and discharging strategies to minimize costs and reduce grid dependency. The EMS integrates with various devices and forecasts to provide real-time optimization.
Read Full Article ‚Üí

Key Takeaways

  • Pramac's EMS offers two specialized modes: "Smart Self-Consumption", which minimizes residual grid purchase costs by charging the BESS during low-price periods, and "External Control", which enables participation in ancillary service markets.
  • The system combines data from meters, inverters, batteries, controllable loads, PV production, and load consumption forecasts to calculate optimal setpoints for each device.
  • Pramac's EMS is designed with a distributed architecture, ensuring local control and security even without connectivity, while the cloud layer handles optimization, historical analysis, and real-time decision-making.
