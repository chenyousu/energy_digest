The SG-DKF approach addresses the challenges of divergence in traditional dual Kalman filtering by introducing a Lyapunov-based analysis and an adaptive dead-zone rule. This method achieves comparable accuracy to existing methods while reducing the RMSE of state of charge estimates.
Read Full Paper ‚Üí

Key Takeaways

  • The proposed SG-DKF method is designed to address the limitations of traditional dual Kalman filtering, which can experience divergence under large initialization errors or model mismatch.
  • A key innovation of SG-DKF is the use of a Lyapunov-based analysis to establish a sufficient stability condition, leading to an adaptive dead-zone rule for parameter updates.
  • The application of SG-DKF to an electrochemical battery model results in significant improvements in state of charge estimation accuracy, reducing RMSE by over 45% under large initial state errors.
