This closed-loop system enables the controller to optimize power system operation by incorporating contextual information and adaptive predictions. The framework's online tuning mechanism ensures task-aware learning and adaption to non-stationary grid conditions.

Key Takeaways

  • InstructMPC integrates LLMs with MPC to generate context-aware predictions, enabling more effective power system optimization.
