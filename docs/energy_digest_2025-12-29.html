Published: December 26, 2025

The approach aims to balance economic indicators with network operating limits.

Key Takeaways

  • The proposed framework employs a mixed integer nonlinear optimization model to jointly select the connection bus and size the distributed generation (DG) capacity while respecting network operating limits.
  • An intelligent genetic algorithm is embedded in a multi-scenario decision framework, which includes three stakeholder scenarios prioritizing losses, voltage quality, or techno-economic balance, as well as additional balanced scenarios generated automatically until convergence.
