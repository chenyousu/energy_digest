The digital twin enables predictive warning capabilities, identifying potential risks in advance based on trend analysis and learned patterns.
Read Full Article ‚Üí

Key Takeaways

  • A two-tier artificial neural network (ANN) was used to train the digital twin system, which achieved high accuracy in predicting PV surface temperature (R2 value of 0.9996) and power output (R2 value of 0.9189).
  • The digital twin framework uses data from 155 physical experiments conducted in a wave tank to simulate system behavior and forecast performance under varying environmental conditions.
  • The system enables predictive warning capabilities, allowing for the identification of potential risks in advance based on trend analysis and learned patterns, rather than merely reacting to anomalies after they occur.
