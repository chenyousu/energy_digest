This legislation is part of the Clean Homes and Energy Affordability Package.
Read Full Article ‚Üí

Key Takeaways

  • California has introduced a new bill that allows small plug-in solar systems to connect directly to household outlets, eliminating utility interconnection and permitting requirements.
  • The proposed law defines balcony solar systems as devices between 400 W and 1,200 W that use integrated microinverters to convert direct current into alternating current for direct connection with home appliances.

CircuitLM: A Multi-Agent LLM-Aided Design Framework for Generating Circuit Schematics from Natural Language Prompts

Source: ArXiv
Published: January 08, 2026

Summary

The article introduces CircuitLM, a multi-agent LLM-aided design framework that generates accurate circuit schematics from natural language prompts. The system addresses the challenges of large language models hallucinating in granular details and producing non-machine-readable outputs. By incorporating a curated component knowledge base and a hybrid evaluation framework, CircuitLM enables reliable circuit prototyping by non-experts.
Read Full Paper ‚Üí

Key Takeaways

  • CircuitLM tackles the challenge of generating accurate circuit schematics from high-level natural language descriptions, overcoming issues with large language models hallucinating in granular details.
  • The system incorporates a five-stage pipeline, including LLM-based component identification, canonical pinout retrieval, chain-of-thought reasoning, JSON schematic synthesis, and force-directed SVG visualization.
  • CircuitLM uses a hybrid evaluation framework called Dual-Metric Circuit Validation (DMCV) to ensure safety and evaluate the system's performance on 100 diverse embedded-systems prompts across six LLMs.
