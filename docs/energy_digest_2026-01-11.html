Exact Continuous Reformulations of Logic Constraints in Nonlinear Optimization and Optimal Control Problems

Source: ArXiv
Published: January 07, 2026

Summary

The article presents a new approach to reformulating logical constraints in nonlinear optimization and optimal control problems, allowing for the direct integration of discrete logic conditions into continuous models. This is achieved through rewriting logical propositions into conjunctive normal form, converting them into max-min constraints, and applying a smoothing procedure that preserves the exact feasible set. The method is evaluated on two benchmark problems and shown to be more efficient than existing binary variable elimination techniques.
