Source: ArXiv
Published: January 29, 2026

Summary

It finds that an enhanced RP formulation can match LAED's dispatch feasible region at a single time step with additional intertemporal deliverability constraints, but this equivalence does not persist under rolling-window operation due to different optimization objectives. The study highlights the limitations of current ramp product implementations and suggests enhancements.
Read Full Paper ‚Üí

Key Takeaways

  • An enhanced RP formulation can match LAED's dispatch feasible region at a single time step when certain constraints are enforced, suggesting potential operational equivalence between the two methods.
  • However, this equivalence does not generally persist under rolling-window operation due to differences in optimization objectives between LAED and RPs.
  • LAED achieves similar or lower load shedding than RP implementations with the same look-ahead horizon, particularly under high-load, ramp-limited conditions.
