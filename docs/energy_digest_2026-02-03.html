By operating in three coordinated phases, DCoPilot achieves near-zero constraint violations and outperforms baseline methods across various DC components.

Key Takeaways

  • DCoPilot utilizes two distinct generative paradigms - a large language model for symbolic generation of structured reward forms and a hypernetwork for parametric generation of policy weights.

  • DCoPilot achieves near-zero constraint violations and outperforms baseline methods across five control task families spanning diverse DC components.
