Solar
Source: PV Magazine
Published: May 30, 2026

Summary

ContourGlobal has commissioned a 231 MW solar photovoltaic plant with a 200 MW / 1.3 GWh battery energy storage system (BESS) in Chile's Tarapacá Region, marking the largest-duration operational utility-scale BESS project in Latin America, providing up to 6.5 hours of continuous energy supply after sunset. EU approves capacity market for Spain

EV
Source: PV Magazine
Published: May 30, 2026

Summary

The European Commission has approved a capacity mechanism for Spain, which will mobilize up to €9 billion over 10 years, starting in May 2026. The scheme aims to ensure sufficient firm and flexible capacity to meet the national reliability standard by remunerating resources during periods of system stress or scarcity. Participants will compete through auctions on the level of remuneration requested per MW of available capacity.
