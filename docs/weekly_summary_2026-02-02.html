Executive Summary

This week's energy industry news highlights the growing dominance of renewables, with FERC reporting that 88% of new US power generating capacity additions to November 2025 came from renewable sources. The increasing adoption of battery storage (BESS) and solar energy is also evident, with Spain deploying 1.13 GW of solar for self-consumption in 2025 and Europe contracting nearly 24 GWh of BESS under flexibility purchase agreements. Additionally, the integration of data centers and AI is gaining traction, with a survey revealing that majority believe renewables and BESS are the ideal energy mix, and utilities are investing in grid modernization solutions to maintain power during severe weather events. These developments underscore the evolving landscape of the energy industry, driven by growing demand for clean and sustainable energy sources.
