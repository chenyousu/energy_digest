The approach aims to address scalability challenges faced by traditional electromagnetic transient models.

Neural Operators for Power Systems: A Physics-Informed Framework for Modeling Power System Components

Source: ArXiv
Published: November 07, 2025

Summary

This article proposes a neural-operator framework for power systems using Deep Operator Networks (DeepONets) to model power system components. The framework, known as Physics-Informed DeepONets (PI-DeepONets), embeds the residuals of governing equations into the training loss to enhance generalization and data efficiency.
