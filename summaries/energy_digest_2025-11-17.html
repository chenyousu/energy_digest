Who Moved My Distribution? Conformal Prediction for Interactive Multi-Agent Systems

Source: ArXiv
Published: November 14, 2025

Summary

The article discusses the challenges of using conformal prediction for interactive multi-agent systems, where uncertainty-aware prediction is crucial for safe motion planning. The authors introduce an iterative conformal prediction framework that adapts to the changing behavior of surrounding agents, providing probabilistic safety guarantees and improving success rates in simulation scenarios.

Key Takeaways

  • Most existing frameworks using conformal prediction assume non-interactive surrounding agents, which can lead to endogenous distribution shifts as uncertainty-aware agents change their behavior.
  • The proposed method addresses this challenge by introducing an iterative conformal prediction framework that adapts to the evolving behavior of reactive, non-ego agents.
  • The framework provides probabilistic safety guarantees and improves success rates in simulation scenarios, with an overall improvement of up to 9.6% compared to other conformal prediction-based baselines.
