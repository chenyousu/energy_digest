This achievement is significant, as it nearly matches the two-hour, two-cycle L1 tariff without viability gap funding. The selected developers will set up the systems on a build-own-operate basis to meet Rajasthan's peak and off-peak energy requirements.

Key Takeaways

  • The projects awarded are eligible for central financial assistance in the form of viability gap funding through the Power System Development Fund scheme issued by the Ministry of Power.
